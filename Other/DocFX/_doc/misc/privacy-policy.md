# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how libreautomate.com ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

### Information We Collect

#### Forum accounts
When you create an account on our forum, we collect:
- Username
- Email address
- Password (stored securely)
- If you choose to register or log in through a social provider (for example, Google), we also receive your name and email address from that provider

#### Cookies
We use cookies to:
- Keep you signed in to your forum account
- Remember your forum settings
- Maintain forum session security

### How We Use Information

We use collected data to:
- Provide access to the forum
- Maintain user accounts and preferences on the forum
- Notify users about new posts on the forum, if they have opted in

### Sharing of Information

The website is hosted by Hostinger and uses the MyBB forum software to operate the forum. These services may have technical access to data but do not use it for their own purposes.

We do not share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or lawful requests from authorities.

### Data Retention and Your Rights

We retain forum account information as long as your account is active.
You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us.
You can edit your personal information in the forum's User Control Panel.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under data-protection laws, such as the right to access your data, request correction, or withdraw consent.

### Security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

### Software Downloads

The software available for download on this website does not collect any personal information from users.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
